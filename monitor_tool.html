How to Use Your iPhone to Monitor Noise

  1. Open the Health app on your iPhone.
  2. Navigate to the Browse tab and select Hearing.
  3. Enable the feature Environmental Sound Levels.
  4. Wear your Apple Watch (if you have one) or use the iPhone's built-in microphone to monitor surrounding noise levels.
  5. View noise levels in real time or check historical data in the Health app.
  6. For more advanced noise monitoring, download apps like dB Meter or NIOSH Sound Level Meter from the App Store.

Monitoring noise helps you measure the impact of engines and determine the effectiveness of noise-reduction strategies.
